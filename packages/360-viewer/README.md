# 360 Viewer – Tactile Product Exploration

Let customers explore products the way they'd hold them in their hands. Drag to rotate. Pinch to zoom. A 360-degree viewer transforms a static product image into an interactive experience that builds confidence and reduces purchase hesitation.

## Why 360 Viewer Matters

**Increases purchase confidence** – Users can inspect products from every angle, just like in a physical store  
**Reduces returns** – Fewer surprises means fewer returns; customers know what they're getting  
**Feels premium** – Interactive product exploration signals quality and attention to detail  
**Compels engagement** – Users spend more time with products they can manipulate  

Perfect for: e-commerce platforms, product showcases, jewelry, furniture, luxury goods, collectibles, real estate listings.

## What You Get

- Intuitive drag-to-rotate interaction on desktop
- Natural pinch-to-zoom on touch devices
- Auto-rotate option for passive viewing
- Responsive design for all screen sizes
- Clean, minimal interface that stays out of the way
- Accessible to keyboard and screen reader users
